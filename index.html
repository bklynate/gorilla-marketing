Our Culture

 

Ask Why?

In your daily work: It is not just okay, but expected that you ask questions, raise issues, and really understand the problem you are trying to solve!

3x2 Decisions

In your daily work: You have the power and responsibility to make decisions according to our 3x2 model: the decision outcome makes sense for your colleagues, clients and the company financials: now and in the future. If you need help you can seek advice, but never approval!

Working in London

In your daily work: You get to work in a perfect balance of having the support of an established company with a mature culture, but still enjoying the benefits of getting to hack and really make your mark in a team that has everything to prove.

Feedback

In your daily work: Giving and receiving feedback is critical for continuous improvement and to achieve our shared goal. It's expected that everyone gives honest feedback to developers, designers, team leaders and even the CEO!
