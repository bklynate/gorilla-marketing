Our Culture

 

Ask Why?

The ”Ask Why” slogan serves as a reminder that no matter how many times you need to ask the same question, you’re obliged to do it until you've reached your goal: the real problem that needed to be solved.

3x2

At Futurice everyone has the power to decide, and the obligation to make good, transparent and fair decisions. To support that, we've created the 3x2 model.

When moving towards a decision or a new project, there are three (3) times two (2) aspects to consider about its immediate and future impact: how does the decision affect your colleagues, the customer and the numbers, now and in the future.

Working in London

London is Futurice's Unicorn Zone.

The Unicorn Zone is what we call the place with a perfect balance of having the support of an established company with a mature culture, but still enjoying the benefits of getting to hack and really make your mark in a team that has everything to prove.

Feedback

Giving and receiving feedback on a daily basis is something that binds us together while working on shared goals. By giving feedback, we show that we care about our shared goal. By receiving feedback, we show that we value our colleagues' and our customers' views.
